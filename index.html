All of us have experienced online shopping at one point of the other but ask yourself , is that experience the same as of a real supermarket? No right. Our product solves that issue.

Virtual Carts

Previous Next

Just like in a real supermarket , we have some virtual carts places at the entrance of our mart that can be picked by simply a mouse click.You're ready to shop now.

Move around the whole store , drag objects from the shelves and place them in the cart.The items added will automatically get deducted from the inventory and added to the bill.You'd be surprised to see how quick the whole process is while maintaining the smoothness of the experience.

Billing Counter

Previous Next

After roaming around the whole mart , do you have something in your cart? Just go to the billing counter and voila , your bill is generated , displayed on the computer and added in that database.

Did we mention , whenever you select a product , all it's details are mentioned on the pc in the counter.It's almost as if the whole mart is giving you all it's attention. Beautiful , isn't it?
